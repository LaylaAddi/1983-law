    Tips for a Good Story
    • Be specific about dates and times - "August 8th at 1:15 PM" instead of "last week"
    • Name the people involved - Include officer names, badge numbers if you know them
    • Describe the location - Include the city, state, and specific location
    • Quote what was said - Include dialogue as accurately as you can remember
    • Describe physical actions - Did anyone push, grab, handcuff, or touch you?
    • Mention witnesses - Were there other people who saw what happened?
    • Note any evidence - Do you have video, photos, or documents?
