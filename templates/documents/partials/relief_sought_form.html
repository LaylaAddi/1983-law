What Your Complaint Will Ask For

Based on standard Section 1983 civil rights cases. These are the typical requests for First Amendment audit cases.

Compensatory Damages - Money for your actual losses and suffering
Punitive Damages - Extra money to punish the officers
Attorney's Fees - Make them pay your legal costs if you win
Declaratory Judgment - Official declaration that your rights were violated
Jury Trial - Have regular citizens decide your case
Policy Changes - Court-ordered changes to department policies
